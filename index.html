Design and Drafting Services

We can provide full 2D & 3D design and drafting services, from simple 2D component drawings to full 3D engineering assemblies for production using conventional methods or rapid prototyping.

We have also produced local building planning drawings for authority approval. This includes extensions, window layouts, roofing systems and much more.

Advice and Consulting

We can produce photo-realistic renderings of designs for visualisation and instruction manuals. We also offer a full legacy drawing conversion service, with the ability to translate almost any CAD format to another.

We also provide an advisory service on CAD systems. If you or your company are confused about what system you need (if indeed you need one) or who to get it from then we can advise. No need to have sales reps only pushing certain systems, with almost 30 years of experience we can advise on exactly what suits you and your business.
