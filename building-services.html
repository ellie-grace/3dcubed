Building Planning

Building Planning Drawings

If you’re thinking of extending your house, have an idea about what you want but don’t really want to go to the time and expense of engaging an architect. We can provide advice and generate drawings for almost any size and type of extension, as well as guidance on the planning process.

Building Planning Advice

You may need full planning permission or be allowed to proceed under permitted development, either way you will probably need drawings for building control. We won’t do the application for you, but we can guide you through the process. We can also arrange for structural engineering approval from a leading practice if required.

Other Services

Have you had ideas and can’t really visualise what it will look like from a technical drawing? Don’t worry, we can provide a 3D CAD visualisation or even a scale model 3D printed on our in house rapid prototype machine. If you are intending to spend tens of thousands of pounds on an extension, knowing what it will really look like in relation to the rest of the property.
